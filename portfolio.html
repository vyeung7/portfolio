Portfolio

Welcome to my work, these are the projects that I created this semester in DIG2000. This course really helped me figure out my strong suits and struggles but overall I enjoyed the process of making each piece.

Photoshop

This photoshop project is my first time really creating graphics using this program. I definitely struggled trying to edit images non destructively but I understand the adjustments I should make. I did enjoy brainstorming the concept I had which was loosely inspired by the videogame Outer Worlds. So I was able to learn how to compile a few images from the internet to create a opening scene that highlights the landscape of just another space movie.

Stop Motion

My stop motion animation was probably my favorite project to do, mostly with designing the cute theme of Lovebirds. He's a country boy and she's a city girl so that's the idea I went with. I took 225 photos and really used the best of my surroundings to help out animate it. I would say that having a tripod would really be useful in between takes but I think I managed to stable my camera to the best of my abilities to make the story flow.

Video Editing

For the video editing project, it was a refresher that helped me brush up my skills and see what has changed with the program since I last used premiere pro. The idea for the video I chose was to do a perfume commercial so I gathered a general idea for the shot list and storyboard from watching various perfume videos. I see the few adjustments I could make given the time and the storytelling could've been cut down on. But I like to make sure I got the idea across. In which, the perfume smells like your living a dream were everything is clean, simple, and just a relaxing selfcare day. Overall, I enjoyed putting together each clip and working with my friend to model in the video.
